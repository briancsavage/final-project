Onym Words: A Vocab Game

Designed by Eric Duanmu, Angela Maliakal, Daniel Lee, and Brian Savage

Featuring the Merriam-Webster Collegiate Thesaurus and Dictionary APIs





How to Play:

1. Two words will be shown. Your job is to select whether the words are synonyms, antonyms, or unrelated.

2. Once you select, you can hover over the word for its definition.

3. When you are ready, click next to get your next pair of words.

4. You have 3 lives. If you get three pairs wrong, you will lose!

Click the continue button to get started!
